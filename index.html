Donate to Help Those Struggling with Mental Health at NAMI

The National Alliance on Mental Illness or NAMI is a foundation dedicated to providing aid for those combating mental illness and suicidal thoughts. Their services include a 24/7 help line, advice on how to handle mental health struggles, programs to raise awareness, and education on this difficult topic. Your money can be directly involved with saving lives and increasing quality of living for people who feel completely alone. If you are looking for a way to help someone change their life, this is the donation to make.
